# United States — Tariff & Trade Remedies Data Sources

## Primary Tariff Authority
- U.S. International Trade Commission (USITC) — HTS
- Data access: https://hts.usitc.gov

## Trade Remedies
- U.S. International Trade Commission (USITC)
- U.S. Department of Commerce (ITA)
- Section 232: U.S. Department of Commerce

## Automation Approach
- HTS data: periodic structured download (annual updates)
- Trade remedies: rule-based flags + periodic checks

## Notes
- No official free real-time API for full HTS
- Automation relies on authoritative published datasets

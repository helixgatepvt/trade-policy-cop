# European Union — Tariff & Trade Remedies Data Sources

## Primary Tariff Authority
- European Commission — TARIC (Integrated Tariff of the EU)
- Data access: https://ec.europa.eu/taxation_customs/dds2/taric

## Trade Remedies
- European Commission Trade Defence Instruments
- Anti-dumping, countervailing, safeguard measures

## Automation Approach
- TARIC: structured data downloads and periodic updates
- Trade remedies: rule-based flags from official notices

## Notes
- TARIC is the single source of truth for EU customs duties
- Prefer official EU datasets over third-party APIs

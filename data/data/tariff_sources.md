# Tariffs — Source of Truth

## India–EU FTA
- European Commission TARIC database
- Official FTA schedules published by EU and India

## US–India MFA
- U.S. Customs and Border Protection (CBP)
- Office of Textiles and Apparel (OTEXA)

## Usage Rule
- Prefer official government-published tariff schedules
- Do not estimate or interpolate tariff rates

## Assumptions
- If tariff data is unavailable or unclear, system flags for manual review
